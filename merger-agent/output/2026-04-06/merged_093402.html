Anthropic Cuts Off Claude Pro/Max Subscriptions from Third-Party Agents Like OpenClaw, Forcing API Pay-As-You-Go

Anthropic מנתקת Claude Pro/Max מסוכנים של צד שלישי כמו OpenClaw, כופפת למודל API בתשלום כשימוש

📅 April 4, 2026

Anthropic ended the ability for Claude Pro ($20/mo) and Max ($100–$200/mo) subscribers to route usage through third-party frameworks like OpenClaw and OpenCode, effective April 4, 2026. Users who want to continue using Claude with those tools must now pay separately under a new 'extra usage' billing model, with API-equivalent costs potentially exceeding $1,000/month — an estimated 5–10x increase over previous subscription workarounds. Anthropic attributed the change to capacity management and differing usage patterns of external tools, but developers widely viewed it as a move to eliminate a quiet subsidy that had made Claude the engine of choice for the open-source AI agent community. The decision generated over 1,077 upvotes and 817 comments on Hacker News, with strong sentiment that the policy penalizes users who adopted Claude through open-source integrations.
