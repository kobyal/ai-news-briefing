Google's AI Edge Gallery app, which enables running Gemma 4 fully offline on Android devices, has graduated from beta to official app stores and no longer requires a Hugging Face account, token, or developer setup to access local models. This significantly reduces friction for non-developer users wanting to experiment with on-device AI. The app currently lacks some Gemini capabilities like email access or system toggles, but represents a meaningful step toward mainstream local AI adoption independent of cloud infrastructure.
