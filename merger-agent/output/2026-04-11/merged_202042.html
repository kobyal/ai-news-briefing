📅 April 8, 2026

Anthropic unveiled Claude Mythos Preview through Project Glasswing, granting roughly 50 vetted organizations access to its most powerful unreleased model for defensive cybersecurity work. Partners include CrowdStrike, Google, NVIDIA, AWS, Apple, and JPMorgan Chase.
