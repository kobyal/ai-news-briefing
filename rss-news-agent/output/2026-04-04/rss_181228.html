📅 April 01, 2026

Research reveals that large language reasoning models encode tool-calling and decision-making patterns in pre-generation activations, detectable via simple linear probes. NearID framework addresses fundamental vulnerabilities in vision encoders that entangle object identity with background context, undermining personalized generation and image editing tasks.
