📅 April 07, 2026

Q-Zoom addresses the inference bottleneck in MLLMs handling high-resolution visual inputs by implementing query-aware adaptive perception instead of global resolution scaling. The approach exploits spatial sparsity and query intent to reduce redundant tokens in the quadratic self-attention mechanism, significantly improving throughput while maintaining performance on fine-grained tasks like document understanding.
