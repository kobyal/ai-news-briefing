Microsoft מסירה את המיתוג של Copilot מאפליקציות Windows 11 ומשיקה Foundry Local SDK

📅 April 13, 2026

Microsoft began scrubbing Copilot branding from Windows 11 applications, replacing it with generic labels like 'Writing Tools' while keeping AI functionality intact, suggesting strategic rebranding rather than capability rollback. The company also released Foundry Local, an SDK for developing AI-powered apps compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux, allowing integration of Qwen and Whisper series models. Additionally, Microsoft launched the Digital Data Council framework for AI governance, addressing the gap between data infrastructure and responsible AI deployment as enterprises scale AI initiatives.
